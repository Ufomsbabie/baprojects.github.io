New Canteen Ordering System

This Capstone Project sought to assist a global FMCG company automate its food ordering system. This was to enable its employees to have the options of ordering their lunches from the canteen to be delivered to their desks, sitting and eating in the canteen, or ordering their meals for collection. This project touched on the Stakeholders involved, the RACI Matrix, As-Is and To-Be Process, the Business Case, User Stories and Jira Process.

One of the world's leading airlines was looking to improve its customer satisfaction and retention rates.
